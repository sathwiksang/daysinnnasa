1 King Bed, Second Floor, Interior Stairs Only

✨ Elevated comfort with exclusive interior stair access
Please Note: This second-floor room is accessible via interior stairs only. Perfect for guests who prefer a quieter, more private setting away from ground-floor activity.

The 1 King Bed, Second Floor, Interior Stairs Only, Non-Smoking at NASA Days Inn & Suites is an air-conditioned room with large and comfortable King bed that can easily accommodate up to 2 adults making this a perfect room for couples. This spacious room offers a 40 inch flatscreen TV w/HBO, coffee maker, microwave, fridge in a wetbar, iron/ironing board, ceiling fan, and a small sitting area with a work desk. Free WiFi is included. The room includes a private bath with full tub and granite vanity with hairdryer and complimentary toiletries and plush towels.
