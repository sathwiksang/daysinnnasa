1 King Bed, Accessible

✨ Thoughtfully designed for comfort and accessibility
Accessibility First: NASA Days Inn & Suites cares about accessibility. This room has been specially designed with comprehensive ADA features to ensure comfort and independence for all guests. Our staff is available to assist with any specific needs.

NASA Days Inn & Suites cares about accessibility and we have designed the 1 King Bed, Accessible guest room for guests with additional needs. This room features a large and comfortable King bed that can easily accommodate up to 2 adults making this a perfect room for couples. This spacious room offers a HDTV w/HBO & Cinemax, coffee maker, microwave, & mini-fridge in a wetbar, iron/ironing board, ceiling fan, and a small sitting area with a work desk. Free WiFi is included. The room includes a private bath with full tub and granite vanity with hairdryer and complimentary toiletries and plush towels. Feel free to contact our staff for more information about this accommodation.

Accessibility Features

Comprehensive ADA-compliant features designed to ensure comfort, independence, and ease of access for all our guests with mobility, hearing, or visual needs.

Room Accessibility Features

  • Wheelchair accessible entrance with 32" clear passage width
  • 36" or more accessible path around and between beds
  • Door hardware usable with one hand without tight grasping
  • Lowered closet bars and shelving for easy access
  • Multi-level door viewers at accessible heights
  • Service animals welcomed

Technology & Controls

  • Closed captioning television
  • Visual alarm equipment for hearing impaired
  • Visual door knocker alert system
  • Telephone with volume control and visual call alert
  • Communication kits with TTY machine available
  • Shaker alarm clock for hearing impaired

Accessible Bathroom Features

  • Wheelchair accessible sink with 29" clearance
  • Raised toilet seat 17"-19" above floor
  • Grab bars at toilet, tub, and shower
  • Hand-held shower wand with 60" hose
  • Tub and shower seats available
  • Slip-resistant bathroom floors

Electrical & Furniture

  • Accessible power outlets 15" from ground
  • Power outlets within 4" of telephone
  • Push button light and lamp controls
  • Lower level individual thermostat
  • Accessible desk height with knee space
  • Drapery controls with lowered wands

Need Additional Assistance?

Our staff is committed to ensuring your stay is comfortable and meets all your accessibility needs. Please don't hesitate to contact us for more information or special arrangements.
