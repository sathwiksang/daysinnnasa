Must-Know Information

  • All guests under 25 years checking in without an adult will be charged a refundable cash deposit of $100
  • Guests residing within 50 miles of the property will be charged a refundable cash deposit of $100 USD
  • All guests must check in by midnight - Please inform us of late arrival in advance
  • Photo ID and credit card are required at check-in for all guests
  • Please inform us of your expected arrival time in advance using the Special Requests box when booking

Check-In Policy

Check-in time is from 3:00 PM to 11:00 PM. Guests must show photo ID and credit card at check-in. Please notify the property of your arrival time in advance. Late arrivals after 11:00 PM must be arranged prior to arrival.

Check-Out Policy

Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be arranged with the front desk based on availability. Additional charges may apply for late departures without prior arrangement.

Children & Beds

Children of all ages are welcome. Children 18 and above will be charged as adults at this property. Cribs and extra beds are not available. Please add the number and ages of children when booking for correct pricing.

Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed at this property. We regret any inconvenience this may cause. Service animals as defined by the ADA are always welcome.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is permitted in designated areas. Please select your room preference when booking. A cleaning fee will apply for smoking in non-smoking rooms.

Cancellation/Prepayment

Cancellation and prepayment policies vary by accommodation type. Please check the conditions of your selected option when booking. Enter your stay dates to see specific terms.

Age Restriction

The minimum age for check-in is 25 years. Valid government-issued photo identification is required for all guests at check-in.

Group Bookings

When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional fees may apply. Please contact the property directly for group reservations and special arrangements.

General Information

Please note the following important information about our property:

  • Our property has no elevator - upstairs rooms are accessible via stairs
  • Security deposits required: Cash deposit of $100 USD for guests under 25 or living within 50 miles
  • 24/7 Virtual Front Desk available for assistance
  • Special requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges
  • We take special requests - add them in the next booking step!
